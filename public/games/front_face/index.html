The game

Front-Face is a simple HTML5 game,
a mix of memory-like style game and trivia about the speakers,
made to promote Front-Trends 2012 conference.

The conference

Front-Trends 2012 is a gathering for Front-End lovers
to discover the current trends to build a professional career
out of innovative front-end development.
The conference will take place in SOHO Factory (Warsaw, Poland)
on 26-27 April 2012.
